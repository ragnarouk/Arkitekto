# Francisco Mañosa

Francisco Mañosa was a Filipino National Artist for Architecture recognized for promoting Filipino identity through design.

## Notable Works
- Coconut Palace
- San Miguel Corporation Headquarters
- Amanpulo Resort

## Design Philosophy
Mañosa championed Neo-Vernacular Architecture, integrating traditional Filipino elements with modern techniques.
