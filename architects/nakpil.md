        <h3><b>Juan Nakpil</b></h3>
        <p>Father of Philippine Architecture</p>
        <p>Juan Nakpil is the first ever National Artist for Architecture in the Philippines which is why he was given the title "Dean of Filipino Architects". He was a pioneering Filipino architect, teacher, and civic leader. He blended Art Deco, modernism, and traditional Filipino elements to shape the nation's pre- and post-war architectural identity. He advocated for a distinct national architectural style that reflected Filipino culture while embracing modernity.</p>
       <h3>Design Philosophy</h3>
       <p>He sought to create a "Philippine Architecture" that blended functionality with local traditions and culture.</p>
