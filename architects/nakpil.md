        <h3>Juan Nakpil</h3>
        <p>Juan Nakpil is the first ever National Artist for Architecture in the Philippines which is why he was given the title "Dean of Filipino Architects". He was a pioneering Filipino architect, teacher, and civic leader, he is celebrated for pioneering a modern Philippine architecture that blended traditional, indigenous elements with modern design.</p>
       <h3>Design Philosophy</h3>
       <p>He sought to create a "Philippine Architecture" that blended functionality with local traditions and culture.</p>
